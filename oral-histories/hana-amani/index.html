About Hana Amani

thecapilanoreview.com/author

Hana Amani was born in Colombo, Sri Lanka in 1994 coming from a Sufi Muslim background. Her family has been in the gemstone trading business, which has passed down for their last 6 generations. The first seven years of her life were spent growing up in Los Angeles, where her father wanted to set up an office.
