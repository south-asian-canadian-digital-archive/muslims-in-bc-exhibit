About Hifza Shahzad

Hifza Shahzad was born and raised in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. At a young age, her family relocated to the city of Mangla, where her father began working for a hydroelectric company called WAPDA. She spent her early childhood there, growing up with her four siblings consisting of three sisters and one brother, while her mother managed the household. For a lot of her childhood she was surrounded by Mangla’s natural beauty, living there for nearly a decade with her immediate family.
